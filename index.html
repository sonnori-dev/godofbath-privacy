By using our Services, you agree to the terms of this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue using the Services.

1. Purpose of Data Collection and Use

We collect and use personal information for the following purposes:

To create and manage user accounts

To operate, maintain, and improve our Services

To provide customer support and respond to inquiries

To analyze usage and improve user experience

To ensure security and prevent fraud or misuse

To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

To send service notices, promotions, or marketing information (with consent)

2. Information We Collect and How We Collect It
(1) Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Services on any platform (VR, console, PC, mobile, or web), we may automatically collect:

IP address, access time, device identifiers, operating system, platform type, app version

Advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA, ADID)

Server logs, gameplay activity, and usage statistics

(2) Account Creation and Social Login

If you log in through external platforms such as Facebook, Google, Apple, or Kakao,
we may receive information you permit those services to share, such as your name, email address, profile picture, social ID, locale, and friends who also use SONNORI games.

(3) Customer Support and Event Participation

When you contact customer support or participate in events, we may collect your email address, account ID, game title, screenshots, device information, or payment details to assist you.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies, SDKs, and similar technologies to identify devices, store preferences, analyze traffic, improve service quality, and measure advertising effectiveness.
You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, certain features may not function properly.
Third-party tools (e.g., Google Analytics) may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Sharing and Data Processing

We may share personal information with trusted partners and service providers who perform services such as hosting, analytics, customer support, and payment processing on our behalf.
Information is shared only as necessary, and we maintain oversight of all data processors.
We may also share data when required by law or with your explicit consent.

5. Use of Personal Information

Collected data may be used to:

Operate and improve our Services

Manage accounts and restore access

Respond to inquiries and provide support

Send administrative notifications

Process payments and deliver virtual items

Prevent fraud and enhance security

Provide promotional or marketing information (with consent)

Conduct analytics and personalize user experiences

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy or as required by applicable laws.
Once retention periods expire or purposes are fulfilled, data is securely deleted or anonymized to prevent recovery.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own, including the Republic of Korea.
In such cases, we take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data remains protected in accordance with applicable laws (e.g., GDPR Standard Contractual Clauses).

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 14 (or 13 where applicable).
If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your Personal Information:

Access and Correction – You have the right to access and correct or update your personal information. In many cases, you can do this directly through your account settings.

Deletion – You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. Once verified, we will process your request within 30 days, subject to lawful extensions. We will retain only minimal records necessary to document your request and our response.

Restriction – You have the right to restrict certain types of processing of your personal information.

Objection – You have the right to object to certain processing activities.

Withdrawal of Consent – You may withdraw your consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on prior consent.

Complaints – You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority in your country of residence or employment if you believe your data rights have been violated.

Requests will be handled promptly after verifying your identity, except where retention is required by law.

11. How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, please contact us by email with the following details:

Your account ID or nickname

The game or service name

The email address linked to your account

Contact: sonnori@sonnori.com

Subject line: “Data Deletion Request”

After verifying your identity, we will delete your data except where retention is legally required.
Unlinking your Meta or platform account alone does not delete in-game data — you must send a request as described above.

Consent (for optional marketing or analytics; may be withdrawn at any time)
